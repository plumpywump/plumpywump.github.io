My Story

Hello, My name is Shwetha Elisha (Pronounced Sh-way-tha El-eye-sha). I have been drawing every since I was tiny, I’m much older now, but still tiny! I grew up in Bangalore, India and now live in the US. I have a background in Illustration, Graphic Design and Fine Art (MFA). In my free time, I love curling up with a good book and a steaming cup of chai. I also love picking up as many art and craft hobbies as I can (my house is littered with the results of my artistic endeavors). I have a hunger for learning and trying out new things.



My Work Ethic

I am a results-oriented, ambitious, hardworking self-starter with a ton of ideas and an eye for design. I’m highly regarded for out-of-the-box thinking, working efficiently, juggling multiple tasks, prioritizing, meeting deadlines and achieving high standards in creative execution.
