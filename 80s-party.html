Music App UI/UX Case Study



Inroducing 80s Party

Objective

The objective of 80s Party is to provide a comprehensive and enjoyable platform for users to access and explore the vast and iconic music catalog of the 1980s. The app aims to create a nostalgic and immersive experience that allows users to relive the music, culture, and memories of the decade through a user-friendly interface.


The Problem

The current music streaming services do not provide a comprehensive platform for users to access and explore the iconic music of the 1980s. Despite the popularity of 80s music, there is a lack of dedicated apps that offer a user-friendly interface, personalized playlists, radio stations, and recommendations based on the user's listening habits. This creates a gap in the market that 80â€™s party aims to fill.


Target Audience

The target audience is adults aged 25-65. Mainly Gen Xers and nostalgic Millennials.
