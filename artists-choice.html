Inroducing Healthy Indian Cooking


Overview

Artists Choice is a responsive web app, that provides a curated online platform for artists and art enthusiasts alike, offering a wide selection of high-quality fine art supplies. We aim to foster creativity and inspire artistic expression by offering a diverse range of art materials that cater to various styles, mediums, and skill levels.

I was the sole designer & researcher for this project and the duration was 1 month.


Objective

We, at Artists Choice are passionate about art and are committed to being a trusted destination for artists to discover, explore, and acquire the finest art supplies that enable their creative journeys.


The Problem

Since many generic art and craft stores only have basic art supplies and brands, Artists Choice aims to solve this problem by providing a wide range of fine art supplies that cater to various styles, mediums, and skill levels targeted solely towards artists.

Logo & Icons


Our logo represents a pencil with paint drips that signify drawing and painting. There are both horizontal and vertical versions of the logo that can be used in different situations. The page icons have two variations: Light and Dark without text that can be used in limited space or mobile applications.
